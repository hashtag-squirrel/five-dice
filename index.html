Rules

How to play:

Click on "Roll" to start the game. Each turn you have 3 rolls of the dice. To "lock" a dice from the roll, click on it and you will see a blue border around locked dice. You can unlock it again at any point. Within a turn, after the first roll, you can decide to end the turn and enter your score in the scoresheet in any empty field. At the end of the turn, the dice will unlock automatically. The objective is to score as many points as possible on the scoresheet.

How the scores work:

Ones to sixes: The sum of dice with the number corresponding the field is counted, e.g. all ones, all twos, etc...

Three of a kind, four of a kind: If you have three of a kind or four of a kind, the sum of all dice is counted. If you don't have three or four of a kind, you get 0 points.

Five of a kind: 50 points if you have five same dice, 0 points if you don't.

Full House: 25 points if you have three of a kind and two of a kind, e.g. three times 3 and two times 1. Can also be filled with five of one kind, but still only awards 25 points. 0 points if you don't meet the conditions.

Small Straight/Straight: 30 and 40 points respectively, awarded for four or five sequential dice, e.g. 1-2-3-4(-5).

Chance: Sum of all dice, no condition.
